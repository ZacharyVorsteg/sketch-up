## üî¢ MEASUREMENT BOX TRICKS

**Don't click in the box! Just type!**

### While Drawing:

| Type This | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| `1000` | Exactly 1000mm |
| `1000,2000` | Rectangle: 1000mm √ó 2000mm |
| `1m` | 1 meter (converts automatically) |
| `3'6"` | 3 feet 6 inches (if in imperial) |

### After Creating:

| Type This | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| `*3` | Make 3 copies (after Move+Copy) |
| `*3x` | Make 3 external copies |
| `*3/` | Divide into 3 parts |
| `5m` | Change last dimension to 5m |

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Keep your left hand on the keyboard** (shortcuts)
2. **Keep your right hand on the mouse** (drawing)
3. **Don't look at the keyboard** (muscle memory)
4. **Practice the same sequence 10 times** (builds speed)
5. **Learn 2-3 new shortcuts per day** (don't overwhelm)
